Access an account - GST/HST

My Business Account

My Business Account is a quick and secure portal to access GST/HST, payroll, corporation income taxes, excise taxes, excise duties and other levies accounts online.

Sign-in/register: My Business Account

Someone else's business account

Representatives (including family members, employees, and accountants) can access My Business Account on behalf of someone else using Represent a Client.

Sign-in/register: Represent a Client

Offering 21 hours of service per day. View hours of service.

To confirm the account number of a supplier, use the GST/HST Registry.

What can I do on My Business Account?

File a return
File returns electronically through My Business Account without using a Web access code (WAC). A WAC is a personalized code that the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) assigns to you to access the secure areas of the CRA's Internet filing Web pages. Along with your return, you can also electronically file any applicable public service bodies' (PSB) rebate claims, new housing rebate applications, and Ontario First Nations point-of-sale relief claims for the same filing period. Form GST106, Information on Claims Paid or Credited for Foreign Conventions and Tour Packages, can also be filed electronically in My Business Account.
View expected and filed returns
View the status of:
  • expected returns (or a message, that no returns are expected); and
  • filed returns covering the last six years.

In addition, if the return has been processed you can view the line-by-line details of the return.

Adjust a return
Select a return from a list of returns that are eligible for adjustment, and enter the adjusted amounts.
File a rebate
These rebates can be filed online:

  • GST66 / GST284, Application for GST/HST Public Service Bodies' (PSB) Rebate and GST Self‑government Refund
    • PSB rebate claims that are filed with a GST/HST return can continue to be filed using the File a return service.
  • GST490, GST/HST Rebate Application for Federal, Provincial, and Territorial Governments
  • GST190, GST/HST New Housing rebate application for houses purchased from a builder (option 1A or 1B only) New
  • GST523, Non-Profit organizations- Government funding form.

If you are a selected listed financial institution (SLFI) that reports both the GST/HST and QST, the QST version of these rebate forms (if applicable) must be submitted using the applicable paper form.

View a PSB rebate
View the:

  • details of an assessed or reassessed PSB rebate
  • status of a PSB rebate

Statuses are: received, assessed, adjustment requested, adjustment denied, and reassessed.

If you are a selected listed financial institution (SLFI) that reports both the GST/HST and QST, this service will only list GST/HST rebate information. Any applicable QST rebate information cannot be viewed online.

Adjust a PSB rebate
Select and revise PSB rebates that have been previously assessed (some restrictions apply).

If you are a selected listed financial institution (SLFI) that reports both the GST/HST and QST, you can use this service to adjust your GST/HST rebate information. To adjust QST rebate information, send your request in writing to the Summerside Tax Centre.

File an election
These elections can be filed online:
  • GST20, Election for GST/HST Reporting Period
  • GST20-1, Notification of Revocation of an Election for GST/HST Reporting Period by a Listed Financial Institution
  • GST70, Election or Revocation of an Election to Change a GST/HST Fiscal Year
  • GST71, Notification of Accounting Periods for GST/HST
  • GST74, Election and Revocation of an Election to Use the Quick Method of Accounting
  • RC4616, Election or Revocation of an Election for Closely Related Corporations and/or   Canadian Partnerships to Treat Certain Taxable Supplies as Having Been Made for Nil Consideration for GST/HST Purposes
  • RC7220, Election for GST/HST and QST Reporting Period for a Selected Listed Financial Institution
  • RC7220-1, Notice of Revocation of an Election for GST/HST and QST Reporting Period by a Selected Listed Financial Institution
  • RC7270, Election or Revocation of an Election to Change a GST/HST and QST Fiscal Year for a Selected Listed Financial Institution
  • RC7271, Notification of Accounting Periods of a Selected Listed Financial Institution for GST/HST and QST
View elections
This page displays information about certain elections that have been processed for the account. If there are more details available about a particular election, they will be displayed as a hyperlink.
Account balance and activities

View an interim balance of payments and credits received for a period end of a return not yet processed and a balance for the result of a return processed for a specific period less held credits and any amounts under dispute not yet applied or not yet due.

Select any hyperlinked period end, balance or total amount to view the corresponding transactions. You can also select the "Account Transactions" link from the left sidebar menu.

Select "Transfer Payment" to transfer a payment/credit from one interim period to another or to an amount owing within and between program accounts of the same 9‑digit business number (BN).

Select "Calculate Future Balance" to view a revised balance that includes interest calculated to a date you select. This option is available on the left sidebar menu if you have an amount owing in a filing period.

Select "Customized View" to view account transactions for a timeframe of your choice that may not coincide with a specific period end. You can also select this option from the left sidebar menu and the "Account Transactions" screen.

Manage direct deposit

As a business owner or representative with Level 3 (Delegated Authority) authorization, you can start, update or stop a direct deposit online.

A Direct deposit can be managed on the following program accounts:

  • RC – Corporate Income Tax
  • RD – Excise Duty
  • RE – Excise Tax
  • RG – Air Travelers Security Charge
  • RN – Excise tax on insurance premiums
  • RP – Payroll Deductions
  • RT – GST/ HST
  • SL – Softwood lumber products export charge
  • RZ - Information Returns
  • RZ  - TFSA
  • RZ  - Contract payments
View direct deposit transactions
This service allows the viewing of direct deposit amounts refunded to a taxpayer’s account for a specific time frame. When the View direct deposit transactions link is selected, a page is displayed that includes a drop-down list for Year/Month/Day where the time frame can be entered. After the starting and ending dates are entered, the "View" button can be selected to see the direct deposit transaction details for that time frame.
Manage address
Change, or view the business addresses online. Select "Manage address" from the applicable account type section or "Manage addresses" from the left sidebar menu. To view the address we have on file for the physical location of the business, the mailing address, and the books and records for one or all accounts, select "View address" from the left sidebar menu.
Calculate your instalment payments and view their due dates
Enquiries service

Make an online request

This service allows a business to make requests for certain financial and non-financial actions online as follows:

Financial
  • request an interest review
  • transfer of a credit
  • request a refund (except GST/HST accounts)
  • initiate a payment search

All of the above requests are processed manually, and each request is promptly forwarded to the appropriate tax centre for action.

Non-financial
  • change mailing instructions (stop the CRA from sending paper statements, remittance vouchers, envelopes, or the GST/HST Tax Return for Registrants)
  • order additional remittance vouchers
  • request copies of notices and statements
  • request customized statements

In most cases, the system processes these requests within 24 hours. If the system is unable to respond automatically, the requests will be sent to a tax centre for processing. If the request requires correspondence to be generated, it will be sent by regular mail.

Submit an enquiry

Ask a question relating to a GST/HST return, GST/HST rebate, corporation return or specialty business returns account.

View enquiry status New

View an originally submitted enquiry, the status of an enquiry, and responses if they are available.
