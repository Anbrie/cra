Who can apply

Typically, three conditions must be met to apply for the Disability Tax Credit :

  • You must have a severe impairment in physical and/or mental functions all or substantially all of the time
  • This impairment should have lasted or is expected to last for a continuous period of at least one year
  • A qualified practitioner needs to certify that your impairment is severe and prolonged by completing the Disability Tax Credit Certificate, (otherwise known as Form T2201)

People of any age can apply. In order to qualify, you must be impacted in one of the following 10 categories.
