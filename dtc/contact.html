Contact us about CRB

Online:

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • apply for the Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)
  • update your address and direct deposit information
Sign in to CRA My Account Register
By phone:

Call the CRA by phone or TTY if you have:

  • have trouble applying for the CRB because you haven’t filed a tax return recently
  • want to make changes to your account (updates to address or direct deposit)
  • have trouble accessing CRA My Account
Before you call:

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
