Contact the CRA

Online:

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • Apply for the Canada Emergency Rent Subsidy (CERS)
  • Update your business information, including your address and banking information
  • Get your personalized account information in My Business Account.
Sign in to CRA My Account Register
By phone:

Call the CRA by phone or TTY if you have:

  • have trouble applying for the CRB because you haven’t filed a tax return recently
  • want to make changes to your account (updates to address or direct deposit)
  • have trouble accessing CRA My Account
Before you call:

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • business number (BN)
  • business name
  • personal and business account open or an assessed return, reassessment, or other tax document
  • business mailing address
