Access your information

My Account

My Account lets you view, change and manage your personal tax information online. You can track your refund, change your return, change your address or telephone number, register to receive online mail, set up direct deposit, and so much more.

MyCRA

The MyCRA mobile app lets you view your return status, change your address, update your direct deposit information, update your marital status, and much more, all on your mobile device.

MyBenefits CRA

The MyBenefits CRA mobile app lets you view your benefit payments and eligibility information on your mobile device.
