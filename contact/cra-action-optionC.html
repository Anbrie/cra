Mistake or delay by the CRA

You have the right to lodge a service complaint or request a formal review of a CRA decision.

If you believe a CRA officer intentionally misled you or mishandled the case, you can submit a reprisal complaint.

Otherwise, file an objection to dispute an assessment or a determination, or appeal a CPP/EI decision.

Lastly, you can file an appeal with the Court if you disagree with the CRA's decision on your objection or your CPP/EI appeal.
