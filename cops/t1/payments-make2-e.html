Make a payment - Personal income tax

Payment options for the following:

  • Personal taxes (T1 – individual income tax) payments
  • Benefits and credits repayments
  • Part XIII – non-resident withholding tax for individuals

There are several ways to pay what you owe. Select the best option for you.

Online
Electronic banking
Description
You can make any payment to CRA through an banking app or website. Most electronic banking lets you schedule a tax debt payment to be taken out of your account at a future date.
Details: Pay by electronic banking online
Processing time
within 5 business days

The payment is applied when it's received by CRA and not backdated to the day you made it

Debit
Description
My Payment is an electronic service that lets you make payments directly to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) using your bank access card. Pre-authorized debit (PAD) is a secure, online self-service payment option for you to pay your personal or business taxes. You can also use a third party provider to pay with Interac e-transfer.
Details:
  • Pay by debit online with My payment or
  • Pay by debit online with PAD or
  • Pay by debit online with Interac e-transfer through a third party
Processing time
1 to 2 business days

The payment is applied when it's received by CRA and not backdated to the day you made it
