Filing by computer

Complete your tax return online with automated software, and send your return online or by mail.

Filing by phone

Answer questions using an automated phone system to file your return.

 Low to modest or fixed incomes and your situation remains unchanged year-to-year Free Generally: Within 2 weeks Automatic
Filing by paper guide

If you do your own taxes using a paper tax return, you must do the calculations yourself.

 Anyone Free Generally: Within 8 weeks Manual
Using a community volunteer tax clinic

Volunteers at atax clinic may be able to complete your tax return for you. Tax clinics are generally offered between February and April across Canada, while some are open year-round.

Those with a low to modest income and a simple tax situation Free Generally: Within 2 weeks Automatic
Using a representative

A representative is a person or business that you authorize to deal with the CRA for you. This person could be your spouse or common-law partner, other family member, friend, or accountant.

Anyone Free or varies Generally: Within 2 weeks Automatic
Using a discounter

A discounter is a tax preparer who calculates your refund, and immediately pays you part of your refund—called a discounted tax refund—before filing your income tax and benefit return with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

 Anyone Varies with some free options Generally: Within 2 weeks Automatic
