Quarterly tax instalments - Personal income tax

Instalments are income tax payments the CRA requires you to pay 4 times a year:

  • March 15
  • June 15
  • September 15
  • December 15

These payments cover the expected taxes owing that you would normally have to pay in a lump sum on April 30 of the following year.

Instalments are not paid in advance, but paid during the calendar year in which you are earning the taxable income.
