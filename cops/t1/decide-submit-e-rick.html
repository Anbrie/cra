Options for doing your taxes - Personal income tax

There are 6 ways to do your taxes.

On this page

  • You complete your own return
  • Someone else completes your return

You complete your own return

Certified software

Use certified software found online, or installed on your computer, mobile or tablet, to automate the completing of your taxes. Your taxes are then sent to us online, by mail, or dropped off.

Who can use
Anyone
Cost
Free or varies
Processing time
Typically within 2 weeks

Someone else completes your return

Representative acting on your behalf

A representative is a person or business that you authorize to deal with the CRA for you. For example, your spouse or common-law partner, a family member, friend, or accountant. Your taxes are then sent to us online, by mail, or dropped off.

Who can use
Anyone
Cost
Free or varies
Processing time
Typically within 2 weeks

Learn more
Representative authorization

Tax preparer that pays part of your refund right away

A discounter calculates your refund, and immediately pays you part of your refund, called a discounted tax refund, before your taxes are assessed by CRA. Your taxes are sent to us online.

Who can use
Anyone
Cost
Varies
Processing time
  • Discounted refund: Immediate
  • Return assessed by CRA: Typically within 2 weeks
