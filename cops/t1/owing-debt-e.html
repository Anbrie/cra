Balance owing on your tax return

If your total payable (line 43500 of your return) is more than your total credits (line 48200 of your return), enter the difference on line 48500. This amount is your balance owing.

You should file your tax return, pay any amounts you owe, or make a post-dated payment to cover your balance owing on or before April 30, 2021 to avoid paying interest and late-filing penalties.

Interest relief if you received COVID-19 benefits

If you received emergency or recovery benefits, these benefits are taxable and must be reported on your 2020 tax return.

Interest relief on 2020 taxes owing will be given to people who have a total taxable income of $75,000 or less in 2020 and received at least one COVID-19 benefit in 2020.

You must file your 2020 tax return to qualify for this interest relief.
