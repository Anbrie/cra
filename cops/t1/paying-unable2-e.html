Unable to pay for individuals - Payments to the CRA

You may be looking for:

  • Arrange to pay your personal debt over time

If you are unable to pay your debt right now or unable to pay it at all, contact the CRA to discuss your options.

  • Call us to discuss your options

Call us to discuss your options

Speak to a CRA agent to see if you can:

  • defer your repayment to a later date when you can afford to pay
  • start or make changes to a payment arrangement
  • discuss other ways to resolve your debt

Consequences of not paying

If you ignore your tax responsibilities and debt, or refuse to co-operate with the CRA to eliminate your debt, the CRA may take legal action.

For details: Consequences of not paying your personal tax debt

Other options if you cannot pay

Options if you cannot pay your debt
Situation Summary More information
Taxpayer relief You may ask for relief from penalties and interest to reduce the amount you owe. Taxpayer relief provisions
Insolvency or bankruptcy If you have no other financial options, you might consider insolvency or bankruptcy. Office of the superintendent of bankruptcy
