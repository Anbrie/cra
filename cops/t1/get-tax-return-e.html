Filed your 2019 tax return on paper?

You will automatically receive your 2020 Income tax package by mail.

Filing exceptions

There are other situations where you are required to file different income tax returns:

  • you have residential ties in more that one province or territory
  • you are filing for someone that died
  • you emigrated from Canada in 2020
  • you resided outside of Canada on December 31, 2020, and:
    • you kept residential ties (factual resident)
    • you were a government employee, or member of Canadian forces or their overseas school staff, or working under a Canadian International Development Agency program, you may be considered a deemed resident of Canada.
    • you stayed in Canada for 183 days or more, and do not have significant residential ties with Canada
    • you did not have significant residential ties with Canada and neither of the 2 previous situations apply to you, you may be considered a non-resident of Canada for tax purposes.
