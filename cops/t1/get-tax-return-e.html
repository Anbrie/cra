Ways to get your tax return

There are several ways you can get the Income Tax Package for 2020, or the Income Tax and Benefit Package for Non-Residents and Deemed Residents of Canada for 2020.

Online

View or download an electronic copy of the Income tax package for 2020.

  • Income tax package for 2020
  • Income tax and benefit package for non-residents and deemed residents of Canada for 2020
By mail

Get a copy of a form and publication:

  • Order alternate formats for persons with disabilities: Alternate formats, including digital (MP3), electronic text (Etext), braille, large print
  • Order copies in regular print format

If you filed your 2019 tax return on paper before November 30, 2020, you should automatically receive your 2020 Income tax package by mail.
