Outreach activities to support organizations

If your organization provides services to certain populations in Canada, the Outreach program at the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) can help. We offer assistance all across Canada to help organizations educate their clients about the benefits and credits they may be entitled to.

Who we support

We support organizations who support certain populations in Canada. They include:

  • Indigenous peoples
  • Newcomers to Canada or refugees
  • Seniors
  • Students and youth
  • Persons with disabilities
  • Housing-insecure, homeless, or individuals staying in a shelter
  • Modest income Canadians

Organizations who support these groups

  • Not-for-profits
  • Boys and girls clubs
  • Libraries
  • Churches
  • Government

What CRA outreach officers can do

  • present an information session to your employees or clients
  • give a presentation at an event you are organizing
  • provide tailored products to help inform the individuals you serve
  • speak or give a presentation at a conference
  • direct your clients on how to access the benefits and credits that they are entitled to
  • do a training workshop - how to get into My Account; do a simple tax return

How to request for outreach assistance

CRA officers are not able to meet in person due to COVID

If you are interested in having assistance from an outreach officer, select your province or territory below, and fill out the request form.

Someone from our regional offices will contact you to arrange an information session tailored to your clients' needs.

How you can help people directly

Become a volunteer (tax preparer)

Host a free tax clinic

The CRA works with community organizations and their volunteers to help eligible individuals by doing their taxes for free, which ensures they get the benefit and credit payments to which they are entitled.

As a temporary measure during the COVID-19 pandemic, community organizations in partnership with the CRA’s Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP) and the Income Tax Assistance – Volunteer Program in Quebec will be hosting free virtual tax clinics.Virtual clinics connect eligible individuals with trained volunteers who will complete and file their tax return by videoconference or phone, for free.
