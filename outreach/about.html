About the Outreach program: Personal income tax

If your organization provides services to vulnerable populations in Canada, the Benefits Outreach Program at the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) can help. We offer in-person visits all across Canada to help organizations educate their clients about the benefits and credits they may be entitled to.

The CRA's outreach officers can:

  • visit your organization to host an information session for your employees or clients
  • display a booth or deliver a presentation at an event you are organizing
  • provide products tailored to the individuals you serve
  • direct your clients on how to access that benefits and credits that they are entitled to
