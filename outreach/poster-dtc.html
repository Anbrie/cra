Poster: The Disability Tax Credit (PDF)
Poster description

The disability tax credit could help you save money

Do you or a family member have a severe and prolonged impairment? Then you might be eligible for the disability tax credit.

Why apply for this credit?

It can reduce the amount of income tax you may have to pay and open doors to many government programs such as the:

Child disability benefit: which is paid with the Canada child benefit for your child

Registered disability savings plan: to help you save for long-term financial security

Canada workers benefit (CWB) disability supplement: for working individuals

And more...

How to apply?

  • Fill out and sign Part A of Form T2201, Disability Tax Credit Certificate
  • Ask an authorized medical practitioner to fill out and certify Part B of the form
  • Send the form to the CanadaRevenue Agency (CRA) either by mail or online through “Submit documents” in My Account

We can help!

The CRA can help you apply for this credit at no charge. For more information, go to canada.ca/disability-tax-credit or call 1-800-959-8281.
