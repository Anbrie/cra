Content types: CRA design system

There are many different content types:

Type Applies to
Explanatory What is it
Instructive How to do it under common circumstances
Supportive How to do it under uncommon circumstances
Transactional Do it now
Assistive How to get help
Reference Exhaustive information
Foundational Acts, policies
Promotional Audience-specific and time sensitive, and has a specific outcome

Some content types have other factors to consider. For example:

  • a campaign would consider marketing principles
  • a learning product would consider learning and performance support principles
  • an application that supports any of the content types, would consider application development principles

However, all content types must adhere to the following foundational principles:

  • 8 information architecture principles
  • 10 visual design principles
  • 6 interaction design principles

These principles that apply to all pages. Any other principles that support the the content, must built on and compliment these foundational architecture and design principles.
