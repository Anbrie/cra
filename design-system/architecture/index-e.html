Architecture: CRA design system

People often use the words information architecture (IA) to mean the menus on websites or in apps, but that’s not really correct. While menus are a part of IA, they’re really only one part of it.

Information architecture is all about organization of information in a clear and logical way. Such organization follows a clear purpose — helping users navigate complex sets of information.
