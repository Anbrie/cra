Eligibility criteria

Claim the credit for yourself

To be eligible for the GST/HST credit, you must meet the following conditions:

    • If you or your spouse or common-law partner don't have a SIN
      • Note: The CRA usually send the GST/HST credit payments on the fifth day of July, October, January, and April.
    • you are at least 19 years old (or are turning 19 within the tax year)
    • If you are turning 19 in the tax year

    • Definitions: Spouse and Common-law

You need all of the above to be eligible for the GST/HST credit.

You are eligible for the GST/HST credit.

You need all of the above to be eligible for the GST/HST credit.
