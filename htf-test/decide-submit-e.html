Ways of doing your taxes - Personal income tax

There are 6 ways to do your taxes, including some free options and others with varying costs.

On this page

  • Certified tax software
  • Authorize a representative
  • Community volunteer tax clinic
  • Discounter (tax preparer)
  • Paper tax return
  • File my return (automated phone line)
  • Related information

Certified tax software

Use certified tax software to calculate your taxes for you. Options include online, downloaded or installed on your computer, mobile, or tablet.

Who can use
Canadian residents
Cost
Can vary or is free
Processing time
Typically within 2 weeks

For details, go to:
Certified tax software

Authorize a representative

Authorize a family member, friend, or an accountant to act on your behalf to complete your taxes and access your tax accounts.

Who can use
Anyone
Cost
Can vary or is free
Processing time
Typically within 2 weeks

For details, go to:
Representative authorization

Community volunteer tax clinic

Find a free tax clinic to have a volunteer complete your taxes for you.

Who can use
People with low to modest income and a simple tax situation
Cost
Free
Processing time
Typically within 2 weeks

For details, go to:
Free tax clinics

Discounter (tax preparer)

A discounter is a tax preparer who calculates your refund and pays you a discounted tax refund right away, before they file your tax return.

Who can use
Anyone
Cost
Varies
Processing time
Discounted refund is paid by the tax preparer right away

For details, go to:
Discounter information

Paper tax return

Complete and file your taxes manually on a paper return.

Who can use
Anyone
Cost
Free
Processing time
May take up to 12 weeks due to COVID-19 paper processing delays

For details, go to:
T1 Income tax package for 2020

File my return (automated phone line)

CRA will send a letter inviting those who are eligible to use this service. You will need to enter your information through the automated phone line to file your taxes.

Who can use
Only people who are invited, have a low or fixed income, and a simple tax situation
Cost
Free
Processing time
Typically within 2 weeks

For details, go to:
File my return
