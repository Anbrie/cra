Access your account - Personal income tax

Online

My Account

A secure portal that lets you view your personal income tax and benefit information and manage your tax affairs online

Represent a Client

A secure portal to access tax information on behalf of individuals.

MyCRA

A secure mobile-friendly site for individuals that lets you securely access key portions of your tax information and pay your tax balance owing.

MyBenefits CRA

A secure mobile-friendly site for individual benefit recipients that lets you quickly view your benefit and credit payment details and eligibility information.

ReFILE

A secure service that lets individuals and EFILE service providers send online adjustments for income tax and benefit returns with certified NETFILE software and EFILE software.

By phone

Tax Information Phone Service (TIPS)

Get both personal and general tax information from our automated phone system.

File my Return automated phone service

Individuals with low or fixed income will be able to file their income tax and benefit returns simply by giving some personal information and answering a series of short questions through an automated phone service.
