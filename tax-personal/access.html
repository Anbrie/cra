Access an account - Personal income tax

Your personal account

My Account is a quick and secure portal to access your personal income tax and benefit information, plus manage your tax affairs online.

Sign-in/register: My Account

Someone else's personal account

Representatives (including friends and family members) can access My Account on behalf of someone else using Represent a Client.

Sign-in/register: Represent a Client
