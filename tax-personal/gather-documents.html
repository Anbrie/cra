Gather your documents - Personal income tax

On this page

  • Documents you will need

Documents you will need

  • your tax slips such as your T4, T5 or T2202A information slips

    Auto-fill my return - A secure CRA service that lets you or your authorized service provider automatically fill in certain parts of your current year return. The CRA will have tax slip information, such as T4, registered retirement savings plan information, and carry forward amounts. For more information refer to Auto-fill my return.

  • information relating to any income not reported on a tax slip
  • receipts for deductions and credits you can claim

If documents are missing

File your return on time even if you do not have all of your slips or receipts. You are responsible for reporting your income from all sources to avoid any penalties and interest that may be charged. If you have not received your slip by early April or have miss placed them, here are some options to retrieve your tax slips.

  Online

If you wish to view or print any tax slips from the past 6 years, including but not limited to employment insurance (EI), old age security (OAS), and Canada Pension Plan (CPP) you can try:

  • Auto-fill my return
  • My Account for Individuals
  • My Service Canada Account (MSCA)
  Phone

Should there be any difficulties accessing your tax slips online you can contact:

  • your employer
  • the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA):
    • 1-800-206-7218 (English service) for EI or
      1-800-808-6352 (French service) for EI
    • 1-800-277-9914 (English service) for CPP and OAS or
      1-800-277-9915 (French service) for CPP and OAS
