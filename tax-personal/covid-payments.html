Overview

The Canada emergency and recovery benefits are new for the 2020 tax year.

Availability dates

both T4A and Relevé 1 slips will be printed by February 28 and mailed out shortly after.

Availability of T4A slips for My Account and Auto-fill my return purposes will begin in mid-January with all slips being available by February 15.

Amending or cancelling the slip

Amended and cancelled slips will be issued by early March in the event that the information reported on the original slip is incorrect.

For example, a benefit repayment made in 2020 was misallocated and only identified after the original slip was issued. An amended slip will be issued if the repayment only covers a portion of all benefits received. Or, if all of the benefits were repaid from 2020, a cancelled slip will be issued.

Repaying the amount

Benefit repayments in 2021 in relation to a 2020 benefit will be reported on a 2021 T4A slip. The corresponding deduction will be available on the 2021 tax return. In this case, the 2020 T4A slip is not amended and the relevant income and tax withheld is required to be reported on the 2020 tax return.
