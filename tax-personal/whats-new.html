What's new for the 2018 tax filing season - Personal income tax

Filter items

Applies to everyone
Province/territory-specific

Search results

Title Tags Location Teaser
Canada Caregiver Credit Tags: 2018 taxes, impairment, disability, impacted health Canada-wide This non-refundable tax credit replaces the family caregiver credit, the credit for infirm dependants age 18 or older, and the caregiver credit. It gives tax relief to eligible individuals who have a spouse or common-law partner, or a dependant, with an impairment in physical or mental functions.
Disability tax credit (DTC) certification Tags: 2018 taxes Canada-wide Nurse practitioners across Canada can now certify the application form for the DTC.
File your taxes over the phone with File My Return Tags: 2018 taxes Canada-wide This new service lets eligible Canadians with simple tax situations file their return by answering a few questions over the telephone through an automated service.
View transactions and pay balances with CRA BizApp Tags: 2018 taxes Canada-wide The CRA has released a new mobile web app called CRA BizApp. This app lets small businesses and sole proprietors view their business account balances and make payments by pre-authorized debit to their corporation, goods and services tax / harmonized sales tax (GST/HST), payroll, and excise duty accounts.
Pay taxes in person Tags: 2018 taxes Canada-wide You can now pay your individual tax, benefits and credits repayments, and other select payments to the CRA in person with cash or a debit card at any Canada Post outlet across the country.
Alberta income tax brackets Tags: 2018 taxes Alberta residents The personal income levels used to calculate your Alberta tax have changed.
British Columbia caregiver amount Tags: 2018 taxes British Columbia residents The former amount for infirm dependants age 18 or older and caregiver amount have been replaced by the new British Columbia caregiver amount.
Manitoba income tax brackets Tags: 2018 taxes Manitoba residents The personal income levels used to calculate your Manitoba tax have changed.
New Brunswick Climate Action Incentive Tags: 2018 taxes New Brunswick residents New Brunswick residents may be eligible to claim the new federal refundable credit for the climate action incentive.
Newfoundland and Labrador tax brackets Tags: 2018 taxes Newfoundland and Labrador residents The personal income levels used to calculate your Newfoundland and Labrador tax have changed.
Northwest Territories tax brackets Tags: 2018 taxes Northwest Territories residents The personal income levels used to calculate your Northwest Territories tax have changed.
Nova Scotia non-refundable tax credits Tags: 2018 taxes Nova Scotia residents The age amount, the basic personal amount, the spouse or common-law partner amount, and the amount for an eligible dependant have increased for individuals with a taxable income of $25,000 or less. The increase is phased out for taxable income between $25,000 and $75,000.
Nunavut tax brackets Tags: 2018 taxes Nunavut residents The personal income levels used to calculate your Nunavut tax have changed.
Ontario tuition and education tax credits Tags: 2018 taxes Ontario residents The Ontario tuition and education tax credits have been discontinued. Students can continue to carry forward unused amounts to claim in a future year.
Prince Edward Island spouse or common-law partner amount Tags: 2018 taxes Prince Edward Island residents The spouse or common-law partner amount and the amount for an eligible dependant have changed.
Quebec Refundable Senior Assistance Tax Credit Tags: 2018 taxes Quebec residents A new tax credit has been created to provide assistance for seniors aged 70 or over on December 31, 2018. It is available for 2018 onward, and you can receive it even if you did not claim it in your income tax return.
Saskatchewan tuition and education tax credits Tags: 2018 taxes Saskatchewan residents The tuition and education tax credits have been discontinued.
Yukon public transit amount Tags: 2018 taxes Yukon residents The Yukon public transit amount has been eliminated.
