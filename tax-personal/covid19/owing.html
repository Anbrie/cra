How you can avoid owing income tax in the future

There are many scenarios which may impact the amount of income tax you have to pay. For example:

  • you were self-employed
  • you have multiple pension or income sources
  • you went on maternity or parental leave and received both a top up and EI benefits

Two ways of avoiding income tax debt:

  • Complete form TD1, Personal Tax Credits Return to allow your employer or payer to deduct additional tax from your pay cheque or your pension.
  • Make voluntary payments to the CRA.
